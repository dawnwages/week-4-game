Crystals Collector

Oh no! You've been hit with a powerful spell attack!

Don't worry, I can help you.

I can tell you the number of crystal points you'll need with given crystal collection to cure you from your spell attack.
But you'll have to guess the right combination to match the crystal points exactly. Good luck.

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
